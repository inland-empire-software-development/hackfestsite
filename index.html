What Is HackFest?

HackFest is a 5-week long event where you and your team collaborate to build an application. The details of the application will be revealed when the event starts. This event is meant to get you introduced in to the culture of the software industry, get you experience working with a development team and provide you opportunities to network.

For additional info, check out HackFest on Meetup.com.

RSVP for the event!

Rules

  • Time limit of 5 weeks
  • Minimum of 2 people per team up to a maximum of 6 people.
  • You can use any library, framework or engine.
  • You must credit all external resources.
  • Good sportsmanship is expected.
  • You cannot use prebuilt code or game kits, but you may use assets
  • Every team must present their project via a 1-3 minute long video.
  • Your project must be submitted as a repository link for record keeping purposes.

Agenda

  • Week 1 – Planning
  • Week 2–4 – Building
  • Week 5 – Polishing and preparing trailer

FAQS

What is a HackFest?

HackFest is a 5 week event in which coders, graphic designers, interface designers, project managers, and others collaborate intensively on software projects.

Who should attend?

All levels of skills and disciplines involved in the software development process are very much welcomed to attend and participate in this event. This includes designers, project managers, documentation writers, researchers, etc. Any skill that can contribute to a team, to complete a project, is a welcomed addition.

What do I need?

This is a hands on technological event. A computer or laptop with any tools that will help you develop your application will be needed, such as a code editor.

What do I gain from HackFest?

You gain experience working in a development team, speaking in a large group and the opportunity to network. Additionally, you challenge your critical thinking and problem solving skills, practice diving in to new code and learn skills from your other team members.

How do I form or join a team?

We do encourage participants to start forming teams before the event. Message members on Slack. Send a message to your peers to find out who is attending and is looking for team members. Don't have one by the day of the event, no problem! Message an operations member on Slack and we will help you find a team. Teams should not be larger than 6.

Will there be prizes?

The winning team will get a guaranteed prize. Prizes change per event.

Who owns my project?

The members of the team that work on your project are the owners of the project. We highly encourage you to share your code with the open source community, but it is not required.

Is there a HackFest code of conduct?

You bet, see it here.

How much time do I have to complete my project?

The event will last for 5 weeks. Additionally, we encourage you to keep working and improving on it after the event. Your imagination is the limit.

Is this event for all ages?

Adults of all ages may participate. Junior and Senior High School students can participate with an adult guardian or chaperone present.
